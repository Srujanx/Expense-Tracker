Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly (the "Service") collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the Service to you. This includes:

  • Account information — such as your name, email address, and password, which you provide when you register;
  • Expense data — the transactions, amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you choose to record;
  • Usage information — basic technical details, such as log data, needed to operate and secure the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve Spendly. This includes displaying your transactions, generating spending summaries and category breakdowns, authenticating your account, and keeping the Service secure. We do not sell your personal or financial information to third parties, and we do not use your expense data for advertising.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored so that it can be made available to you whenever you sign in. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in limited circumstances: when required by law or legal process, to protect the rights and safety of Spendly or its users, or with service providers who help us operate the Service and are bound to handle your data confidentially.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You retain ownership of the expense data you enter. You may access, update, or delete your information within the application at any time. If you close your account, your associated personal data will be removed in accordance with applicable law, except where we are required to retain certain information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the Service or for legal and regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Service after any changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us through the support channels provided within the application.
